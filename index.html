Who We Are

Where Cultures
Meet & Resonate

Origin Forge Pty Ltd is an Adelaide-based media production company with a singular purpose: to bridge the Australian and Chinese communities through the power of storytelling and authentic conversation.

We believe the most compelling content emerges when cultures intersect. Our productions create spaces for thinkers, builders, and visionaries from both communities to share ideas, challenge assumptions, and inspire one another.

From our home in South Australia — one of Australia's most dynamic and multicultural cities — we produce English and Chinese-language content that is intellectually rich, warmly human, and genuinely cross-cultural.
